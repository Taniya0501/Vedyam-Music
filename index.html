ABOUT ME

Having 8 plus years of experience in playing piano and a passion since the beginning and an everlasting yearn for the art, I have performed with much appraise and encouragement in various events including concerts and College fests. I have been a producer for the last two years, currently producing music at Mix with Vasudev studios.
Apart from india I have overseas clients from US Australia and a few more. Having produced music in Hindi, English and Punjabi, I am also deft in video editing, specialising in fine cinematographic edits of music videos, fetching vlog edits etc.
I am also currently working as a video editor of Mix With Vasudev studios, Rohan Bharti and many studios of high volume.
I have performed at timeless theatres including Fidoriya, Purushottam and auditoriums like annabhau sathe etc.
